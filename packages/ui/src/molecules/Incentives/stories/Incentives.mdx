## Components

The `Incentives` uses the [Compound Component](https://kentcdodds.com/blog/compound-components-with-react-hooks) pattern, its components are:

- `Incentives`: the wrapper component.
- `IncentivesItem`: the component that wraps an item of the `Incentives`.

## Props

`Incentives` and `IncentivesItem` components support all attributes also supported by the `<div>` tag.
